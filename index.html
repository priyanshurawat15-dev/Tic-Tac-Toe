How to Play

Phase 1: Placement

Each player places exactly 3 pieces on the board, taking turns.

Phase 2: Movement

Players take turns moving their pieces to adjacent empty cells.

Movement includes all 8 directions: up, down, left, right, and all 4 diagonals.

Winning

Get 3 of your pieces in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) to win!
